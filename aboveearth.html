What the project was about:

This was my first theme project and the first website that I have developed. The project was to develop a concept for a fictional space travel program. Ergo, we had to develop a concept and a digital solution in relation to the sale of travel into space.

Given target group:

The given target group in this project were private individuals who can afford space travel. From here, we had to further define the target group.

roles:

As this was our first project, we did not distribute project roles, however, there were things in the project that I was specifically responsible for and did for myself, such as problem analysis, idea generation, idea selection, sketches and some logo and concept development.

Target group after our research:

Our target group in this project was primarily people from the blue segment of the minerva model. These are people who are materialistic and modern and who are employed in the private sector. Preferably people that earn more than the average resident. For that reason we think that our target group is people such as rich people, businessmen and- or celebrities.

Our concept:

Above Earth is a luxury space company that gives you the opportunity to experience space and visit other planets than Earth. By booking a trip through Above Earth, you get the opportunity to experience space in a completely different way than ever before. You get the possibility to see what the different planets have to offer. Through Above Earth you get the opportunity to experience various spa experiences, romantic trips and getaways to different planets. You will have a high class experience that you will never forget. It will be possible to use our #LetUsTreatYouLikeGods to create promotion and make famous people aware of our brand. Through it, we will be able to show the luxury we are trying to create for our guests. At Above Earth, we offer a lot of different luxurious experiences and take care of everything, so that our guests do not have to think about anything other than enjoying their trip in space. We make sure that everything is easy and prepared, depending on what our guests need. If you are also tired of living on the same boring planet, and you lack excitement and luxury in your life, then we clearly think that you should consider one of the space trips we offer here at Above Earth.
